Participation/Motivation

Assessment (Participation / Motivation): Very good

Comment:
Chloé usually comes to class in a good mood and is generally willing to take part in the activities proposed. She is open to new tasks and, once reminded of the classroom routine, she joins in with enthusiasm. At the beginning of some sessions, she could be a little loud or repeatedly ask for confirmation, but she usually settles well when given clear guidance. Chloé is cooperative with the teacher and with her classmates, and she especially enjoys hands-on activities such as worksheet work, bingo, cards and dressing Emma.

Learning process

Assessment (Learning process): Satisfactory

Comment:
Chloé's English knowledge is satisfactory. She can repeat the new structures well and often understands the language being worked on in class. This term, she worked on colours, clothes, body parts, actions, rooms and places, as well as the concepts of clean/dirty and dry/wet. She can usually tell her age and gender and, on several occasions, used the complete structure correctly. She was also able to pair actions with the correct rooms and take part in the story-time activities. However, she still needs more practice with some vocabulary items, especially clothes and colours, and she sometimes needs the teacher's help to remember or apply the full structure. With more regular revision, she will continue to gain confidence and accuracy.

Behaviour

Assessment (Behaviour): Good

Comment:
Chloé is a lively and generally well-integrated student. She can be a little distracted or impatient at times, especially when she is tired or when the group is especially active, but she responds well to reminders and class management strategies. Once the rules are clear, she behaves appropriately and stays focused on the task. She usually treats the material carefully and works well with the rest of the group. Her attitude in class is positive and she is able to adapt well to the pace of the lesson.

Final comment

Throughout the term, Chloé has shown a positive attitude towards the classroom routines and has taken part well in the activities. She is able to understand and repeat a good part of the language worked on in class, and she is becoming more confident when using complete structures. She still needs to keep reinforcing some vocabulary, especially clothes and colours, but she is making good progress. Well done, Chloé! It is recommended to keep listening to the audio tracks and revising the vocabulary at home in order to consolidate the language learnt during the term.
